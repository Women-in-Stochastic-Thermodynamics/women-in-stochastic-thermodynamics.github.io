Women* in Stochastic Thermodynamics

We are a group of women in Stochastic Thermodynamics and related research areas. Our goal is to create a platform for scientific exchange, networking, collaboration, and mentorship. Through this group, we also aim to increase the visibility of women in this field and to promote diversity and inclusion within the broader scientific community.

We believe that regular meetings to connect with other female* researchers can be highly beneficial for career development and for attracting more people from underrepresented groups to our research community. To this end, we host informal meetings once per quarter, each including both a scientific part and a non-scientific part. In the latter, we discuss topics such as the experience of being part of an underrepresented group in academia, career development, and other relevant issues.

Everyone—from students to early-career and senior researchers—is warmly invited to join. Please feel free to spread the word within your local networks!

Moreover, if you are a researcher outside of the group, we invite you to share your job offers, invitations to scientific events, talks or other opportunities, through our mailing list.

Join

If you are interested in joining our group, please sign up for our mailing list:
  1. Email women-in-stochastic-thermodynamics+subscribe@googlegroups.com
  2. You will receive an email with the subject “Join request for women-in-stochastic-thermodynamics”
  3. Reply to that email, do NOT click the “Join This Group” button in the email itself
  4. The organizers will approve your request

Or get in touch with the organizers: organizing-women-in-st@googlegroups.com

Philosophy

*While we refer to “women” and “female” for the sake of simplicity, we explicitly and warmly welcome everyone who identifies as a woman, non-binary, or gender-diverse. Moreover, all our meetings and events are open to anyone interested in the topics we discuss, regardless of gender identity. Our goal is to foster inclusion, not exclusion.
